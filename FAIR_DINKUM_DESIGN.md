# Design Proposal: Fair Dinkum Publishing & Sovereign Aussie Workflows

## 1. Brand Identity: "Fair Dinkum Publishing"
*   **Vibe**: Professional but casual, trustworthy, "no-nonsense," rugged yet tech-forward.
*   **Color Palette**: 
    *   `Ochre Red` (#A3432B) - Earthy, outback feel.
    *   `Eucalyptus Green` (#4F7942) - Natural, Aussie bush.
    *   `Wattle Gold` (#FFC000) - Bright, optimistic.
    *   `Charcoal` (#333333) - Strong, professional text.
*   **Typography**: Bold, rugged sans-serif for headings (e.g., 'Archivo Black') and clean, readable sans-serif for body (e.g., 'Inter').

## 2. Landing Page Structure (Sovereign Aussie Agent Workflows)

### Section A: The Hero (The "Big Smoke")
*   **Headline**: "Get 10 Hours of Your Week Back. Fair Dinkum."
*   **Sub-headline**: "The complete guide to automating your Aussie trade business with AI. No fluff. Just the cordless drill of the 21st century."
*   **Primary CTA**: "Snag the Ebook - $49"
*   **Visual**: A high-res mockup of the Ebook titled "Sovereign Aussie Agent Workforce Workflows."

### Section B: The Problem (Sunday Night Spreadsheet Hell)
*   Visual of a messy kitchen table with receipts and cold coffee.
*   Copy: "You didn't become a sparky to be a data entry clerk. Stop being a slave to the spreadsheet."

### Section C: The Solution (The Digital Ute)
*   **Diagram 1: The Digital Ute Flow**
    *   `Voice Capture (The Natter)` -> `AI Brain (ChatGPT/Claude)` -> `Action (Quotes/Receipts/Emails)`.
*   **The Three Core Power Tools**:
    1.  **The Quoting Machine**: Turn a 30-second voice note into a professional quote in 15 mins.
    2.  **The Receipt Wrangler**: Kill the 'Shoebox of Doom' with automated OCR and AI categorization.
    3.  **The Virtual Apprentice**: Let AI handle the grumpy emails and the daily run sheet.

### Section D: The "Secret Sauce" (The 14-Agent Workforce)
*   **Diagram 2: The AAW Pipeline** (Based on `AAW_MANIFEST.md`)
    *   A flowchart showing Research -> Writing -> Design -> Web Dev -> Deployment.
*   Explain how this book was *actually built* using the same workflows it teaches.

### Section E: Sovereignty & Security
*   "Your data stays your data."
*   Highlighting Local AI (Ollama/LM Studio) and Australian-centric context (NCC, WHS).

## 3. The Ebook Store ("Fair Dinkum Publishing")

## 4. Technical Implementation
*   **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router).
*   **Styling**: Tailwind CSS 4.0.
*   **Animations**: Motion (for smooth transitions between "Before/After" states).
*   **Icons**: Lucide-React.
